Regrowth

by The Garmers Market

The Garmers Market is a collective of high fashion enthusiasts that source rare and abstract items of clothing to both exibit to the community and to seller to the current market. We research each product we collect and go the extra mile in comparison to other wholesalers to ensure the story the designer wanted to convey with each piece is presented and handled with the upmost respect.

Regrowth is a project being run by the team at The Garmers Market to provide styling services from industry professionals that will be easy to intergrate into anyone's wardrobe on a budget that works for you. We will work with you to suggest products from both our own archive as well as trusted third party sellers that can produce outfits for whatever occassion you need.

We will also provide guidance on how to wear the clothes in your wardrobe in ways you may not have considered previously, whilst also supporting you with social media guidance so you can ensure the outfits we have worked on can be correctly presented to get the biggest boost in response on your posts.
